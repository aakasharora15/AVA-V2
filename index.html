FAQ
Everything you need
to know about AVA.
What is AVA and how does it work?add
AVA is a multi-agent adversarial intelligence platform. Three specialised AI agents engage on your venture hypothesis — The Saboteur audits internal weaknesses, The Predator models external attack vectors, and The Orchestrator synthesises both into a Forensic Verdict with a Resilience Score from 0 to 100.
How is AVA different from asking ChatGPT?add
Generic AI gives you validation. AVA is built to break your idea. Each agent has a distinct adversarial mandate — The Saboteur actively finds internal death-traps, The Predator models how competitors destroy you. No single LLM will take these positions without specialised architecture.
What does the Resilience Score mean?add
The Resilience Score (0–100) reflects how defensible the venture is against both internal failure and external attack. Most ventures score 30–60. Scores above 80 are reserved for genuinely differentiated architectures with strong structural moats. The score is designed to be honest, not encouraging.
Can I download the audit as a PDF?add
Yes. Every audit generates a branded PDF with a dark cover page, AVA logo, venture metadata, Resilience Score, and all 10 sections formatted for sharing with co-founders, investors, or advisors.
How long does an audit take?add
Typically 8–12 seconds. All three agents run in parallel and results stream into the dashboard sequentially. The more detail you provide in the form, the sharper and more specific the output.
Is my venture hypothesis kept confidential?add
Yes. Your hypothesis is processed in real-time and not stored or used for training. Each audit is ephemeral by default.
