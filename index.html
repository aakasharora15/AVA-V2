FAQ
Everything you need
to know about AVA.
What is AVA and how does it work?add
AVA is a multi-agent adversarial intelligence platform. When you submit a venture hypothesis, three specialised AI agents engage sequentially — The Saboteur audits internal weaknesses, The Predator models external attack vectors, and The Orchestrator synthesises both into a Forensic Verdict with a Resilience Score from 0 to 100.
How is AVA different from asking ChatGPT to analyse my business?add
Generic AI gives you validation. AVA is built to break your idea. The three-agent architecture means each agent has a distinct adversarial mandate — The Saboteur is actively trying to find internal death-traps, The Predator is modelling how competitors would destroy you. No single LLM will voluntarily take these positions without specialised prompting.
What does the Resilience Score mean?add
The Resilience Score (0–100) reflects how defensible the venture architecture is against both internal failure and external attack. Most ventures score between 30–60. Scores above 80 are reserved for genuinely differentiated architectures with strong structural moats. The score is designed to be honest, not encouraging.
Can I download the audit as a PDF?add
Yes. Every audit generates a branded PDF report with a dark cover page, the AVA logo, venture metadata, Resilience Score, and all 10 sections formatted for sharing with co-founders, investors, or advisors. The Accelerator tier includes white-label PDF export.
How long does an audit take?add
Typically 8–12 seconds. All three agents run in parallel and the results stream into the dashboard sequentially, so you see each agent's findings appear as they complete. The more detail you provide in the form, the sharper and more specific the output.
Is my venture hypothesis kept confidential?add
Yes. Your hypothesis is processed in real-time and not stored or used for training. Each audit is ephemeral by default. Enterprise and Accelerator plans include additional data handling agreements on request.
